# Business Layer

- **Owner:** Jose AM Talavera
- **Last updated:** 2026-05-16

> Defines the **WHY**. Nothing below this layer is decided until the why is clear.

## Purpose

Establish why the project exists, who it serves, what success looks like, and
under what rules it must operate. Outputs of this layer are the only valid
inputs to the Architecture layer.

## Components

### Vision
- What problem are we solving and for whom?
- One-sentence vision statement.
- 12-month and 3-year horizon.
- **Artifact:** vision statement, north-star metric.

### Product Strategy
- Target segments, positioning, differentiation.
- Build / buy / integrate decisions at the product level.
- Pricing and monetization model.
- **Artifact:** strategy one-pager, roadmap themes.

### Requirements Engineering
- Functional requirements (user stories / use cases).
- Non-functional requirements (performance, availability, compliance, scale).
- Explicit out-of-scope list.
- **Artifact:** prioritized requirements backlog with acceptance criteria.

### Stakeholders
- Who decides, who is consulted, who is informed.
- Internal owners per domain; external partners.
- **Artifact:** stakeholder/RACI map.

### Governance / Compliance
- Legal, tax, data-protection (GDPR), industry obligations.
- Audit and record-keeping requirements.
- **Artifact:** compliance checklist with owners.

### Project Management
- Delivery method, milestones, cadence.
- Risk register, dependency tracking, single backlog location.
- **Artifact:** plan, milestone dates, backlog system of record.

## Inputs
- Market signal / customer demand / feedback from a previous cycle.

## Outputs (to Architecture Layer)
- Vision statement, prioritized requirements (FR + NFR), constraints,
  compliance obligations, success metrics.

## Definition of Done
- [ ] Vision statement agreed and written.
- [ ] Functional + non-functional requirements prioritized with acceptance criteria.
- [ ] Out-of-scope explicitly listed.
- [ ] Stakeholders and decision owners identified.
- [ ] Compliance obligations enumerated with owners.
- [ ] Delivery plan and single backlog established.
